Why LifeSignal Exists

Every day millions of families ask the same question.

"Is my parent okay?"

Many seniors live independently.

That independence is important — but it can also create worry.

What if something happens and no one knows?

LifeSignal was created to solve that problem.

A simple daily check-in confirms everything is okay.

If someone misses a check-in, trusted contacts are alerted.

It’s a small signal that can make a big difference.

Because every life deserves to be noticed.

— The LifeSignal Team
